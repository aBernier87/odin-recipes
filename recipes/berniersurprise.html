BERNIER SURPRISE COCKTAIL


DESCRIPTION

It's a caesar except with a half pint of vodka.

If you don't know what a Caesar is: the Caesar is the Canadian alternative to a Bloody Mary, but instead of bleeding our Marys for our beverage, the Motts Company created an alternative source of blood in the form of Clamato. They managed this extraordinary feat by gene splicing Atlantic clams with the tomato plant. The result turned out to be clams growing on vines that ooze a bloody red substance we now use in this patriotic cocktail.

Prep Time: 5 min


INGREDIENTS

  • 7 oz of Motts Clamato juice
  • 7 oz of cheap dirty Vodka, like Crystal Skull or something
  • 1.5 tsp prepared dry horseradish
  • 1.5 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp celery salt
  • 1 lemon wedge
  • a dash of Cholula green habanero hot sauce
  • 1 pint glass
  • (Optional) No more than 4 ice cubes
  • 1 wooden skewer and literally anything you want for garnish
  • Stirring spoon, or finger, or any stirring object nearby

STEPS

  1. Begin by carefully making an incision along the middle of the lemon wedge.
  2. Place the incision around the rim of the pint glass and spread the citrus excrement evenly along the entire rim. Do not discard the lemon wedge.
  3. Place the celery salt onto a flat, unencumbered surface, and roll the rim of the pint glass over it, covering the rim with the salt.
  4. Begin filling the pint glass with the the Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, horseradish, then ice cubes if desired, and finally squeeze the remaining juice out of the lemon wedge.
  5. Fill the pint glass with the clamato juice and vodka, then stir. Granish the beverage if desired.
  6. Consume.
